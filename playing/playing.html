When we train:

  • Thursday (8.50pm-10.30pm) and Saturdays (5pm-7pm) during full term at the Rosenblatt pool , Oxford University Sport, Iffley Road, Oxford, OX4 1EQ.
  • Mondays (9pm-10pm) at Reading Central Swimming Pool, Battle Street, Reading, RG1 7NU.
  • Tuesdays (9pm-10pm) at Queen Anne's School, Henley Road, Caversham, RG4 6DX.

Club members are welcome to turn up to as many or as little sessions as they want!


To receive details of training sessions, subscribe to our mailing list by sending a blank email to octopush-subscribe@maillist.ox.ac.uk and follow the instructions in the response. Alternatively see our google calender, or join our facebook group!

Getting involved:

When can I play?
  • We are open to both members (students and staff) and non-members of the university!
  • We run taster sessions at the beginning of Michaelmas (1st) Term, but new members are welcome throughout the year at our Iffley Road (Rosenblatt pool) sessions. If you want to come down to one of our sessions, don’t hesitate to get in contact with a committee member and we’ll show you the ropes!
Do I need anything?
  • We can provide all the kit you’ll need to play, so all you’ll need is your normal swimming costume and towel (though obviously players who already own kit are encouraged to bring it!). You may also wish to bring a pair of socks to help with fin fitting.
How much does it cost?
  • In order to play at the Rosenblatt pool you must be a member of the pool - remember, if you are an Oxford University student, your college may well pay this for you!
  • Club membership if £5 per term, which covers club and kit costs as well as the club’s membership to the British Octopush Association (BOA).
